Personal Longevity Project

Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 14, 2026

1. Scope and operator

Personal Longevity Project is a private, non-commercial, single-user application. It is operated solely for the personal use of its owner. The application owner and the only intended data subject are the same person. The application is not offered to third parties.

Privacy contact: proof.topside_2h@icloud.com.

2. Data processed

Depending on the integrations enabled by the owner, the application may process:

  • Oura account profile information and authorized Oura health and wellness data, including sleep, readiness, activity, heart rate, workouts, sessions, tags, stress, resilience, SpO₂, cardiovascular age, VO₂max, and ring configuration;
  • nutrition, body weight, and resistance-training data imported from owner-provided MacroFactor exports;
  • manually entered body weight, blood pressure, supplement adherence, goals, and subjective wellbeing information;
  • Apple Health data only if a future HealthKit integration is explicitly enabled by the owner;
  • technical records necessary for security and reliability, such as synchronization times, source record identifiers, error information, and encrypted authorization credentials.

The application does not request the Oura email scope.

3. Purposes

Data is processed only to import and organize the owner's information, calculate personal trends and data-quality indicators, prepare private fitness and wellness summaries, maintain secure integrations, and support owner-requested deletion or export.

4. Legal basis and consent

The owner explicitly authorizes each connected source through its consent interface and may revoke that authorization at any time. Because this is a private single-user application, no data concerning another person should be entered or connected.

5. Storage and security

During development, application and health data is stored locally on a device controlled by the owner. OAuth credentials are encrypted at rest. Access controls, transport encryption for external connections, data minimization, and source-specific permissions are used where applicable. No health data is stored on this public legal-information website.

If private cloud hosting is enabled later, this policy will be updated before health data is transferred, including the hosting location and relevant service providers.

6. Sharing and artificial intelligence

The application does not sell personal data, use it for advertising, or disclose it to other users. Data obtained through the Oura API is not provided as input to third-party artificial-intelligence models. Any future AI processing of Oura data will occur only through a method permitted by Oura's then-current terms and after this policy is updated.

GitHub Pages hosts only this public legal-information website and receives no health or integration data from the application.

7. Retention and deletion

Health and fitness records are retained only while needed for the owner's requested longitudinal analysis. Authorization credentials are retained only while the integration remains connected. The owner can revoke Oura access and request deletion at any time. Upon deletion or termination, associated personal data and credentials will be deleted, subject only to short-lived technical backups where applicable.

8. Choices and rights

The owner may access, correct, export, restrict, or delete locally stored data and may withdraw source authorization at any time. Questions or deletion requests can be sent to the privacy contact above. Authorization for the Oura integration can also be revoked through the relevant Oura account controls.

9. Medical disclaimer

The application provides personal wellness and fitness information only. It does not provide medical diagnosis, treatment, or emergency services.

10. Changes

This policy will be updated before material changes to data sources, hosting, recipients, AI processing, or availability to additional users. The effective date above identifies the current version.
